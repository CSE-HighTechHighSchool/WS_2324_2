    What is clean energy?

    • Clean energy has no carbon footprint and emits no greenhouse gases
    • It can come in many different forms, including wind, solar, water, geothermal, nuclear, and bioenergy
    • There are also emerging forms of clean energy, like tidal
    • These forms of energy have various perks and drawbacks, but they can all help
    • While some of these can only be done on a more industrial scale, many of these strategies can be implemented at a local level
